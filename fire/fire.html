Fire cannot burn it, Water cannot wet it. The Wind cannot dry it.
Verse 23,Capter 2, The Gita

These charcoals reflect the power we can draw from inside us when everything in the enviroment goes pear shaped.

In 2012, When the doctor told Shivani Mathur to avoid using oil paints because the vapours emitted when the paint was drying were eroding her cornea she traded a box of oil paints for a stick of charcoal.
