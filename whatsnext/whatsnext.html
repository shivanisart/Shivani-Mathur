Shivani Mathur


presents
"The Door"

12 October - 15 December 2023
William gallery, William Blake House, 8 Marshall Street, London.
To join the preview on 12 october please send a message.

The William Gallery, London, is located in the iconic William Blake House, the birthplace of poet William Blake. Curated by Anand Saggar and Nick Woolff, Shivani Mathur presents a work in two-parts - an oil painting and a digital generative artwork that invites us to contemplate the role of our choices in shaping our destiny when we encounter a closed door. Join us at The William Blake House, 8 Marshall Street, London, UK. 12th October to 15 December 2023.
