The Door, 2023 122x92 cm

"The Door" is a two-part artwork, featuring an oil painting and a dynamic moving image of a door. The continuation in pattern on the closed door and its surroundings makes us wonder what is really closed - the door or what we see? It prompts us to ponder on the power of our choices in shaping our destiny.

Hologram of our universe, 2023

"Hologram of Our Universe” is an image of our expansive and ethereal universe that appears to float in mid-air. It is the play of light through a fractured mirror. In the gallery, this hologram was installed in the ceiling. Inspiration gor thisd work comes from quantum physics and the timeless wisdom contained within the Upanishads, an ancient Indian text dating back five thousand years. Both of these sources delve into the mysteries of our universe’s origin and the perpetual cycle of birth, destruction, and rebirth that shapes the fundamental structure of our universe. "

Portrait of our universe, 2023

"Portrait of Our Universe" is a digitsl image of a sculptural work that is encased in a deep box frame, and hung like a painting. It is made from a three-dimensional cracked mirror. The mirror's distinctive topology is crafted using the force of eggshells within a vacuum, resulting in a three dimensional network of cracks that act as pathways for light to shine through. The light seems to connect the cracks and highlight their depth - It's as if the boundaries between "you" and "me" blur, making our individual identities indistinguishable from the magnificent portrait of our universe.
