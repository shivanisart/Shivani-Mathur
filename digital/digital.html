"Portrait of Our Universe" is a three dimensional cracked mirror, encased in a box frame, and hung like a painting. Light shines through the cracks which appear as a web of interconnections. It is as if the boundaries between you and me blur when the light shines, makimg our individual identities indistinguishable from the magnificasnt portrait of our universe. .
