The Door, 2023 122x92 cm.
View

"The Door" is a two-part artwork, featuring an oil painting and a dynamic moving image of a door. The continuation in pattern on the closed door and its surroundings invites us to question what is really closed - the door or what we see? It draws attention to the power of our choices in shaping our destiny.
