Shivani Mathur

Shivani Mathur's current work, Pathways, at the intersection of art, science and technology, was created while listening to the background sound of our universe as recorded by NASA. You can hear it when you touch the images below.




The Door, 2023
Oil on canvas. 122x92 cm.

"The door" is a two-part artwork, featuring an oil painting and a digital generative work that invites us to contemplate the role of our choices in shaping our destiny when we encounter a closed door.




Expansion, 2021
Digital generative art

'Expansion' is a digital generative visualization - we have the entire universe within us.




Rock, Paper, Scissors. 2022
Digital generative art in a collaborative performance.

A three-way collaboration: James, a violinist begins playing music. Kelly Briggs responds to James' music by drawing. An algorithm by Shivani picks up Kelly's body movements and creates a digital image of snow dispersing on a screen, to which James responds by altering his music...




Hologram of our universe, 2023
Cracked mirror and light. 35cmx35cm.

"Hologram of Our Universe‚Äù is made from cracked mirror with colored light streaming through the cracks. It is like an image of our expansive and ethereal universe floating in mid-air on the ceiling in the gallery.




Portrait of our universe, 2023
Cracked mirror and light. 35cmx35cm.

"Portrait of Our Universe" is a three-dimensional cracked mirror, encased in a box frame, and hung like a painting. Light shines through the cracks which appear as a web of interconnections. It is as if the boundaries between you and me blur when the light shines, making our individual identities indistinguishable from the magnificent portrait of our universe.
