Portraits of our Universe, 2022

Portrait of our Universe is a diptych. The image on the left is a physical 3D model, 33x33x10 cm, made with broken mirror and eggshell. The second part of the dyptich, is digital moving image (right). In real life,the materiality of the two makes them look very different, the white lines is light shining through cracks in the mirror,. Both works will be in identical frames hung side by side.
